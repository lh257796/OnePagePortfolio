About Me

Medical Student → Software Engineer

My path into tech is a non-conventional one. Halfway through medical school I decided to take a leave of absence and learn how to code. Several online classes later, I realized that I had a passion for software development. This led me to enroll in a 17-week immersive coding bootcamp in NYC. Upon graduation, I worked on an exciting project in the crypto sphere, which piqued my interest in blockchain development. Currently, I am a fullstack engineer at a fintech company in NYC.
